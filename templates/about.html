Space Biology Knowledge Engine

Space BioSearch is an initiative under the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025. Our mission is to make space biology data accessible, visual, and meaningful. Enabling researchers to explore life’s behavior beyond Earth with clarity and precision.

Our Vision

In an era where humanity prepares for lunar habitats and Mars exploration, understanding biological adaptation in microgravity is critical. We’ve designed Space BioSearch to simplify NASA’s biological datasets into actionable insights bridging data science and space biology.
